The History of HATPI

HATPI was conceived as a revolutionary astronomical project capable of capturing the full night sky in a single 45-second exposure. The journey began with groundbreaking collaborations and generous funding from esteemed organizations.

Key Science

The HATPI project focuses on exploring the dynamic universe, observing phenomena such as transiting exoplanets, supernovae, variable stars, and near-Earth asteroids. Its unprecedented imaging capabilities have opened up new horizons for astrophysical research.
